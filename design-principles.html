Fitt's Law

Spotify

Spotify makes it easier to hit “Play” than other buttons. Fitt’s law stipulates that the time required to move to a target area (e.g. click a button) is a function of the distance to the target and the size of the target. In other words, the bigger an object and the closer it is, the easier it is to use.
