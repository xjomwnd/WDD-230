Target Audience

The target audience for my site are different people such as families and individuals ranging from the age of 18 going up. Their income ranges from $20 000 and their education is from High School and up. This group could include farmers who rely very much on the weather in order to make decisons about their day to day operations.

The site also targets those who love outdoor activities like hiking, ice skating, ski biking, jogging and so foth. Both residence and non residence of Idaho will benefit from this Website, it could be tourists planning to spend some time on a vacation or it could be a resident checking the weather in order to prepare for work.

Personas

Southeast Idaho High Country has members from each community in Region 5 that come together and meet on a monthly basis.

The mission is to promote travel and tourism increasing the state bed tax collections by highlighting and promoting the things to do and see in the region prolonging consumers stay in Idaho creating heads in beds which is ITC's goals for this grant program.
