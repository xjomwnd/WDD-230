Site Name & Domain

southeastidaho.com

The site name is going to be "Southeastern Idaho Regional Report"

The domain name is a simplified name of the site, to enable ease of use by users and also to make it easy for them to find the site. This domain promotes useability.

Logo

The logo is a symbol of the region which has beautiful summer sun in the sky.

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to be the primary and reliable source of weather information to those in Idaho as well as anyone else outside, who might have an interest or plans of visiting the state. The page will include information on local cities, events, and the weather for the area.
