About Bountiful Foods

We manufacture large range of processed and preserved consumables made out of fresh fruits and vegetables. Ours is a fully integrated factory where we procure high quality fruits and vegetables directly from farmers and then do all the processing at our own facility, be it preparing of raw materials, processing and preserving them for further use, or manufacturing end products using these intermediary products.

Make It Your Own

We are an ISO 22000:2005 certified plant with a capacity of producing 2 tonnes of sauce per day. We boast of our pulping facility for raw materials, which have the capacity to pulp 4 tonnes of raw materials per day. Being a sauce manufacturer, we also have a cold-storage facility that helps us store our in-house pulp.
