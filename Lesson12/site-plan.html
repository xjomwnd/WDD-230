    Purpose

    The purpose of Bountful Foods Processing, handling and storage is to prevent foodborne illness and injury, to safely preserve and package foods, and to transport foods from across the globe to our homes. We ensure toxin removal, preservation, easing marketing and distribution tasks, and increasing food consistency.

    Audience

    Our targeted audience in Bountful Foods are people interested in our food varieties. They are mostly people of high and low income and students from various institutions. Individuals working in a stressful environment, such as lawyers, directors, and more, are the target audience. The tenseness that comes when they are feeding bountiful foods, will make them appreciate the satification and relax. Target audience are invited for orders through their phones or email.
