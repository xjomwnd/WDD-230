Preston Pride

Unlike most of the other communities of Cache Valley which were originally settled by immigrating groups of Mormon Pioneers, Preston developed first as an outlying farming area bordering more desirable lands and then arose in time as Cache Valley's second largest city due to its more natural location as a center for smaller communities.
