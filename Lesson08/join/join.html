Membership Level

This membership is for non profit organizations. There is no fee for this membership.







The Bronze Membership is $60 for a year. This membership allows you to access 1 free training a month as well as a 10% event discount.







The Silver Membership is $80 for a year. This membership allows you to access 2 free trainings a month as well as a 15% event discount.







The Gold Membership is $100 for a year. This membership allows you to access 4 free trainings a month as well as a 20% event discount and advertising on the home page.

Gold membership is for businesses with more than 250 employees that are for profit. Additional benefits include:

  • 50% discount on events
  • 50% discount on advertising
  • Free participation in the trade fair
  • 5 featured ads per year
  • 1 free training per quarter
