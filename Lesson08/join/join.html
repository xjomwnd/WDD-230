Membership Levels

Nonprofit

No fee

Nonprofit membership is for businesses that are not for profit and are not self-employed.

Bronze

$50/month

Bronze membership is for businesses with less than 50 employees that are for profit. Benefits include:

  • 15% discount on events
  • 10% discount on advertising

Silver

$100/month

Silver membership is for businesses with less than 250 employees that are for profit. Additional benefits include:

  • 50% discount on events
  • 50% discount on advertising
  • Free participation in the trade fair

Gold

$250/month

Gold membership is for businesses with more than 250 employees that are for profit. Additional benefits include:

  • 50% discount on events
  • 50% discount on advertising
  • Free participation in the trade fair
  • 5 featured ads per year
  • 1 free training per quarter
