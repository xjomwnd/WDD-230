Fitt's Law

Mix

mix.com

Fitt’s law stipulates that the time required to move to a target area (e.g. click a button) is a function of the distance to the target and the size of the target. In other words, the bigger an object and the closer it is, the easier it is to use.

Hick's Law

Apple

apple.com

Apple's Home page presents user's with essentially 3 choices; "Learn More", "Buy", or scroll to the next product. Generally only one product is shown on the screen at the time making the decision even easier. It doesn't take long for the user to decide which link they're going to follow.
